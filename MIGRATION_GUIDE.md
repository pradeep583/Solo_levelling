## 📊 New System Features

### 5-Stat System
- **STR** (Strength): Boost from combat dungeons
- **AGI** (Agility): Boost from agility dungeons  
- **VIT** (Vitality): Boost from endurance dungeons
- **INT** (Intelligence): Boost from intelligence dungeons
- **SEN** (Sense): Boost from sense dungeons

### Rank Progression
```
E-Rank (Starting)
    ↓ [99 Levels × 100 XP each]
D-Rank (200 XP per level)
    ↓ [99 Levels × 200 XP each]
C-Rank (400 XP per level)
    ↓ [99 Levels × 400 XP each]
B-Rank (600 XP per level)
    ↓ [99 Levels × 600 XP each]
A-Rank (1000 XP per level)
    ↓ [99 Levels × 1000 XP each]
S-Rank (2000 XP per level)
    ↓
AWAKENED (Endgame)
```

### Level-Up Rewards
- +1 to all 5 stats
- +10 Max HP
- +5 Max MP
- +1 Free Point (distribute to any stat)

### Rank-Up Rewards (Every 99 Levels)
- Advance to next prestige rank
- +Bonus to all stats
- +50 Max HP
- +25 Max MP
- +1 Skill Point
- HP/MP fully restored
