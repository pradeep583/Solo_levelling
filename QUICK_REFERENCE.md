## Stat Allocation (Level-Up)
- **Free Point**: Distribute to any stat manually
- **Increasing Stat**: Costs 1 point per 1 stat
- **Strategy**: Focus on high-damage stat for dungeons

## Daily Quest Completion

### Morning Routine
1. Open app → Get 6 available dungeons
2. Pick 1-2 dungeons that boost your weakest stat
3. Check them off to gain XP + stats
4. Tips: Spread across different dungeon types

### Expected Daily Gains
- **If completing 1 dungeon**: +30-55 XP, +10-15 stats
- **If completing 2 dungeons**: +80-110 XP, +20-30 stats
- **If completing 6 dungeons**: +255 XP, +60-90 stats

## Strategic Tips

### For Fast Progression
- Complete 6 dungeons every single day
- Focus on one stat type to maximize growth
- Mix combat dungeons for large stat gains

### For Balanced Build  
- Rotate through all 6 dungeons
- All stats grow evenly
- More flexible for future skills

### For Survival (Endurance)
- Prioritize Healing Sanctuary + Endurance
- Build high VIT for boss battles
- Maximize HP/MP recovery
